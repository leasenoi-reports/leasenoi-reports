Where you're losing leases

Specific gaps observed during testing. Each item is grounded in measured behavior, not inferred.

    Five comp-set properties — Vitri, The Beverly, Kota, Broadstone Scottsdale Quarter, and The District — each delivered a human reply within 1 minute. Aspendale's 30-minute human response time trails that benchmark by a wide margin, giving faster competitors first-mover advantage.

    When a follow-up question was submitted during testing, Aspendale did not reply. Failing to respond to a direct prospect inquiry signals disengagement and risks losing renters who expect two-way dialogue before committing to a tour.
