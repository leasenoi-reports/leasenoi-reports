Executive Summary

Best-in-set performance with strong speed, persistence, and multi-touch follow-up.

Our evaluation of IMT North Scottsdale’s leasing system showed the strongest overall performance in the current benchmark set, with an 84/100 LeaseNOI score and a #1 ranking. The biggest remaining opportunity is channel expansion, particularly adding SMS/text engagement to an otherwise strong leasing response system.
