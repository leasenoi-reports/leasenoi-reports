Where you're losing leases

Specific gaps observed during testing. Each item is grounded in measured behavior, not inferred.

  • Weak Sales Conversion After Contact

    Personal engagement scored 10/25 and tour push scored 5/15, indicating agents are responding quickly but not converting that momentum into scheduled tours. This is where rental decisions are won or lost.

  • Follow-Up Lacks Personal Touch

    Of 6 total follow-ups, only 1 was personal — the remaining 5 were automated. When a prospect does not reply, a personal follow-up carrying a direct tour ask is significantly more likely to re-engage them than another automated message.

  • No Reply Engagement Detected

    The Nurture Engine scored 0/5 on follow-up reply engagement, meaning the team did not detect or respond to prospect replies within the follow-up sequence. A 25-hour average cadence across 6 touches also suggests the sequence could be tightened in the early hours.
