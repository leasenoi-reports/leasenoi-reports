Where you're losing leases

Specific gaps observed during testing. Each item is grounded in measured behavior, not inferred.

  • First Human Contact Delayed ~16 Hours After Prospect Reply

    The initial reply was sent by 'Birdie, Camden's Virtual Assistant,' not a leasing agent. A personal reply from John arrived 971.9 minutes (~16.2 hours) later—well outside the window when prospect intent is highest.

  • Tour Invitations Lacked Specific Dates or Times Across 4 Pushes

    Tour pushes appeared in all 4 follow-up touches, but none contained concrete dates or available time slots. Vague tour invitations reduce booking conversion and contributed to a tour_push score of only 5/15.

  • Low Personal Engagement Score (10/25) Limits Nurture Effectiveness

    Personal engagement scored 10/25, reflecting low reply engagement in the Nurture Engine cycle. Of 3 total follow-ups, 1 was automated; reply engagement remained shallow despite a reasonable 7.86-hour average cadence.
