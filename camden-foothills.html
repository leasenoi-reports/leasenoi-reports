Where you're losing leases

Specific gaps observed during testing. Each item is grounded in measured behavior, not inferred.

  • Top 5 Comps Beat Camden's Human Reply Time by 16+ Hours

    Five competitors in the same North Scottsdale Class A market delivered a first human reply in 1 minute. Camden Foothills took 16.2 hours. A prospect who hears from a human agent first is far more likely to tour that property.

  • Low Personal Engagement Compounds the Speed Problem

    Sales Engine scored 15/40 (37.5%), reflecting weak personal engagement alongside tour push behavior. Four tour pushes were logged, but without timely human contact those pushes arrive after prospect interest has likely moved elsewhere.

  • Nurture Relies Too Heavily on Automation

    Nurture Engine scored 12.5/20 (62.5%), the strongest of the three engines. However, the single automated follow-up and 7.86-hour average cadence show the nurture load is still leaning on automation rather than personal outreach.
